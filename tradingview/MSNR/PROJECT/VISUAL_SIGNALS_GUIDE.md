## 🟢 Long Entry Signal (Green Triangle)

### Visual Appearance
```
Price Chart:
                                    
    4320 ────────────────────────── R [0] (Resistance - Target)
                                    
    4310 ──────────────────────────
                                    
    4300 ──────────────────────────
                                    
    4294 ══════════════════════════ S-GAP [0] (Support - Entry Level)
         │                          
         │  ┌──┐                    
         │  │  │                    
         └──┘  └──                  ← Candle wicks to 4294, closes at 4296
            ▲                       
         LONG S-GAP                 
         R:R 2.0                    ← Green triangle appears HERE
                                    
    4275 ══════════════════════════ S [0] (Support - Stop Level)
```

### What It Means
- **Entry**: Price wicked down to support at 4294
- **Close**: Candle closed ABOVE the level at 4296
- **Signal**: Support held, potential bounce opportunity
- **R:R**: Risk 19 points (4294-4275) to gain 26 points (4320-4294) = 1.4:1

### Your Action
1. ✅ Verify candle pattern (pin bar, hammer, engulfing)
2. ✅ Check volume (should be increasing on bounce)
3. ✅ Confirm trend (preferably bullish)
4. ✅ Enter LONG at 4294-4296
5. 🛑 Stop loss at 4274 (below 4275 support)
6. 🎯 Target 1 at 4320 (resistance)

## 🔵 Breakout Up Signal (Blue Arrow)

### Visual Appearance
```
Price Chart:

    4320 ──────────────────────────
                                    
    4313 ══════════════════════════ R-GAP [1] (Was Resistance)
                ┌────┐              
                │    │              ← Candle CLOSES above 4313 at 4316
                │    │              
    ───────────┴────┴──────────────
            ⬆ BO UP                
            R-GAP 4313.64           ← Blue arrow appears HERE
                                    
    4300 ──────────────────────────
```

### What It Means
- **Breakout**: Price closed ABOVE resistance at 4313
- **Level Status**: Now becomes fresh [0] again
- **Potential**: May act as NEW SUPPORT on pullback
- **Caution**: Could be false breakout

### Your Action - Option A (Fade the Breakout)
1. ⏳ Wait for price to pull back to 4313
2. 👀 Watch for rejection (wick touch, closes below)
3. 🔴 If red ▼ appears → SHORT opportunity
4. 🛑 Stop above breakout high (4318-4320)

### Your Action - Option B (Trade Continuation)
1. ⏳ Wait for price to pull back to 4313
2. 👀 Watch for support (wick touch, closes above)
3. 🟢 If green ▲ appears → LONG opportunity
4. 🛑 Stop below 4313

## 📍 Proximity Alert (No Visual on Chart)

### How It Works
```
Price Chart:

    4300 ──────────────────────────
                                    
    4294 ══════════════════════════ S-GAP [0] (Target Level)
         ↑                          
         │ 5 points                 ← Proximity distance
         ↓                          
    4289 ──────●────────────────── Current Price
                                    
         📱 Alert: "Price approaching key level at 4289.00"
```

### What It Means
- **Warning**: Price is within 5 points of a key level
- **Purpose**: Give you time to prepare
- **Action**: Open chart and watch for actual touch

### Your Action
1. 📊 Open the chart
2. 👀 Identify which level is being approached
3. ⏳ Wait for actual wick touch
4. 🎯 Look for entry signal (green ▲ or red ▼)
5. ⚠️ Don't enter early - wait for confirmation

## 🎯 Signal Quality Indicators

### High Quality Signals (Take These)

**Green ▲ or Red ▼ with:**
- ✅ Fresh level [0] or [1]
- ✅ R:R ≥ 2.0
- ✅ Strong candle pattern (pin bar, engulfing)
- ✅ High volume
- ✅ Trend alignment
- ✅ HTF level confluence
- ✅ Multiple level types at same price

**Example Label:**
```
▲ LONG S-GAP R:R 2.5
[0] 4H 4294.00
```
This shows: Fresh [0], HTF (4H), excellent R:R

### Medium Quality Signals (Use Caution)

**Green ▲ or Red ▼ with:**
- ⚠️ Touch count [1]
- ⚠️ R:R 1.5-2.0
- ⚠️ Average candle pattern
- ⚠️ Normal volume
- ⚠️ Ranging market

**Example Label:**
```
▼ SHORT R R:R 1.6
[1] 15m 4313.00
```
This shows: One previous touch [1], CTF only, minimum R:R

### Low Quality Signals (Skip These)

**Green ▲ or Red ▼ with:**
- ❌ Expired level [2]
- ❌ R:R < 1.5
- ❌ Weak candle pattern
- ❌ Low volume
- ❌ Against major trend
- ❌ Low liquidity session

**These signals should NOT appear** (filtered by settings)

## 📱 Mobile Trading Tips

### Quick Glance Identification

**On mobile device, look for:**

1. **Color**:
   - Green = Long
   - Red = Short
   - Blue = Breakout up
   - Orange = Breakout down

2. **Position**:
   - Below candle = Long or Breakout up
   - Above candle = Short or Breakout down

3. **Shape**:
   - Triangle (▲▼) = Entry signal
   - Arrow (⬆⬇) = Breakout

4. **Text**:
   - "LONG" or "SHORT" = Entry signal
   - "BO UP" or "BO DOWN" = Breakout
   - "R:R X.X" = Risk:Reward ratio

### Mobile Alert Response

**When alert arrives on phone:**

1. 📱 Open TradingView app
2. 👀 Find the signal marker
3. ✅ Quick checklist:
   - Is it green ▲ or red ▼? (entry signal)
   - Is R:R ≥ 1.5?
   - Is candle pattern strong?
4. 🎯 Place order if all checks pass
5. 🛑 Set stop loss immediately

## ❓ FAQ - Visual Signals

**Q: Why don't I see any triangles?**
A: Check settings - "Show Entry Signal Triangles" must be ON, and signals must meet minimum R:R threshold.

**Q: Can I get both ▲ and ▼ at same level?**
A: No, you'll get one or the other depending on if it's support (▲) or resistance (▼).

**Q: What if I see ⬆ then ▼ quickly?**
A: Breakout failed. The ▼ is a short signal on the rejection. This is actually a good setup.

**Q: Do signals repaint?**
A: No. Signals appear on bar close and do not change. What you see is what you get.

**Q: Can I hide breakout signals but keep entry signals?**
A: Yes. Disable "Show Breakout Signals" in settings.

**Q: How do I know which level a signal is for?**
A: The label text shows the level type (S-GAP, R, etc.) and price.

**Q: What if multiple signals appear at once?**
A: Rare, but possible if multiple levels are clustered. Trade the one with best R:R and freshness.
