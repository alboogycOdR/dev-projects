### **🎯 The Philosophy: What is The Midnight Reversal?**

Markets don't move in straight lines. Before a significant move, price is often engineered to "induce" traders into taking the wrong position, building up liquidity that can then be used as fuel.

The Midnight Reversal strategy is designed to identify this exact pattern. It operates on a simple, two-phase principle:

1.  **Phase 1: The Trap (Inducement):** First, we identify a clear but weak move *against* the dominant trend. This is the market showing its hand, luring in unsuspecting traders. We see this as a "trap" being set.
2.  **Phase 2: The Spring (Reversal):** Once the trap is set, we patiently wait for the *true* institutional momentum to re-enter the market, confirming the reversal. This is our signal to "spring" into action, trading *with* the real move and *against* the trapped traders.

This model is most effective during periods of lower volume and consolidation, making it ideal for the **"Midnight Killzone"** (early SAST morning), particularly on assets like **Gold (XAUUSD)**.
