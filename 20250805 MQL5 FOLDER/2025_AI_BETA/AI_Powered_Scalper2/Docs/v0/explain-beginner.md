### Step 1: The Scout Gathers Intelligence

Every minute, as soon as a new 1-minute candle closes on your EUR/USD chart, the **Scout** immediately springs into action.

It doesn't just look at that one candle. It gathers a full intelligence report, noting things like:

*   **The Current Situation:** What did that brand-new candle look like? Was it a strong push up (bullish)? A hard drop down (bearish)? Did it have long wicks, showing indecision?
*   **Recent History:** What has been happening for the last 20 minutes? Was the market trending up, down, or just moving sideways?
*   **The "Energy" Level:** How much trading activity (tick volume) was there? Was the last move powerful and backed by lots of trades, or was it weak and unconvincing?

### Step 2: The Scout Phones The Brain

The Scout can't make a decision with this information. That's not its job.

So, it instantly packages this entire intelligence report into a neat, organized message and sends it over a private, high-speed connection (a phone line, if you will) to **The Brain**.

The message says something like: *"Okay, Brain, here's the latest from the EUR/USD front. The last candle was a strong push up. Volume was higher than average. The last 20 minutes have been a slow grind upwards. What are my orders?"*

### Step 3: The Brain Does Its Magic

This is where the "AI" part really shines. The **Brain** receives the report from the Scout and starts its analysis. It's not just looking at one or two simple rules like a normal robot would. It thinks on a much deeper level, just like an experienced professional trader:

1.  **Pattern Recognition:** It looks at the sequence of candles and asks: *"Does this pattern look familiar? I've analyzed millions of historical patterns, and this looks like a high-probability breakout setup."*
2.  **Confirmation:** It then checks the "energy" (the volume). *"Yes, the volume is high. This confirms that the breakout has real power behind it. It's not a fake-out."*
3.  **Context is Key:** The Brain also knows about important price zones. It asks: *"Is this happening near a major level, like a daily high or a big round number (e.g., 1.0800)?"* This helps it decide if the move has room to run or if it's likely to bounce off a wall.

### Step 4: The Brain Creates a Complete Battle Plan

Based on its deep analysis, The Brain doesn't just say "Buy!" or "Sell!". It sends back a complete, precise plan of attack.

The plan might look like this:

*   **Signal:** `BUY`
*   **Entry Point:** `Enter the trade exactly at 1.0805.`
*   **Safety Net (Stop Loss):** `If the trade goes against us and hits 1.0798, get out immediately to limit the loss.` (This is a smart stop loss, placed based on the current volatility, not just a random number of pips).
*   **Profit Target (Take Profit):** `If the trade goes in our favor and hits 1.0815, take the profit and close the trade.`

### Step 5: The Scout Executes the Order Perfectly

The Scout on the front lines receives this battle plan from The Brain.

Because the Scout is a robot, it has zero fear, zero greed, and zero hesitation. It doesn't second-guess the plan. It instantly places the trade in MetaTrader with the exact entry, stop loss, and take profit levels provided by The Brain.

And then, the whole process repeats on the next candle.
